Hashbrowns

McCain Hashbrowns

Diced hashbrowns cooked from frozen

Ingredients

  • Frozen hashbrowns
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oil in frying pan set to medium heat
  2. Add frozen hashbrowns
  3. Add onion powder
  4. Cook until golden brown, stirring occasionally
  5. Add salt
  6. Serve with Ketchup
