Simple Pancakes

Easy Pancakes made with Bisquick

Ingredients

  • Bisquick Mix
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat griddle to medium-high
  2. Pour 2 cups of Bisquick mix in mixing bowl
  3. Add 1 cup of milk
  4. Add 2 eggs
  5. Add 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  6. Add 1 tsp of cinnamon
  7. Add 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  8. Stir until ingredients are combined
  9. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto griddle
  10. Flip when pancakes bubble and edges dry
